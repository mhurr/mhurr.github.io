About Me

Intersectional Boundary Breaker

I was raised just outside of Washington, D.C. and have lived there up until my move to Stanford University, where I am currently pursuing a B.S. in Symbolic Systems and a life filled with unconventional adventure. Since I was young, I was curious. From uncovering patterns in human behavior to speculating about the cosmos, I could never stop learning. I'm currently exploring the interaction between humans and machines. How does social media changing the way we interact? What are the ethical applications of artificial intelligence? How can we redesign to make for a better world?

These are all big questions I seek to find answers to. While these are serious questions, I don't take myself seriously all the time. In my free time, you can find me rocking out on my guitar, making memes, or traveling the globe in search of the world's best street food.

Come explore with me!
